Probability, Statistics and Random Processes

This popular engineering textbook by T. Veerarajan provides a clear and structured introduction to probability theory, statistics, and random processes. The book is widely used by engineering students and covers topics such as probability laws, random variables, statistical measures, probability distributions, hypothesis testing, queueing theory, and random processes.

It includes numerous worked examples, exercises, and explanations that help students understand the mathematical foundations of uncertainty and data analysis used in engineering and computer science.
